Invest in peer tutoring and help your students succeed

Your child is unique. They need someone who speaks their language, understand how they feel, and provide just the right amount of push and support to help them succeed in school and beyond. They need a local high school peer tutor who...

  • Provides your child with positive and motivated role models to encourage them to succeed academically
  • Enhances your child’s social skills and content retention through peer-peer discussions
  • Facilitates a comfortable non-authoritarian learning environment for your child
  • Provides substantial benefits for children with cognitive and behavioral disabilities

You have a lot of work to do. Tap into your school’s hidden potential through a peer tutoring program. Let us help you connect your students with fantastic local role models. With peer tutoring, your school will...

  • Provide free, trustworthy, and on-demand support for students anytime
  • Offer more volunteer hours for tutors, less after hours for teachers
  • Build a stronger community of motivated learners
  • Bring an amazing personal and professional development opportunity to your students
  • Increase school engagement and sense of belonging

Sustainable and Accessible Peer Tutoring

"We are creating a sustainable and systematic peer tutoring model for schools, so one-on-one, in-person, peer tutoring can be provided by trained high school tutors as a free after school resource for all students. Together with technology, PeerSquared simplifies the experience for students while generating actionable insights for stakeholders."

Roger Moore - Principal of Desert Vista High School
