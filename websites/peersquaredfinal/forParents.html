For Parents

Your child needs help with school work, but there are too many resources to choose from.

PeerSquared brings you with a local high school tutor. Not only does they know the content, but they knows how your child is learning it in school. Now, your child has the perfect support to ace that test.

Bring home (virtually) a new friend for your child.

  • Trained local high school peer tutors with a track record of successfully helping other students
  • Amazing role models from the school you’ll probably attend
  • Available to help whenever your child is doing homework or studying for a test
  • Tutors keep all that they earn
  • Click Try PeerSquared to schedule a free session or Learn More below

PeerSquared works in K-12 schools to make peer tutoring a free resource for all students.

  • Absolutely FREE
  • Qualified tutors trained to help your child succeed
  • Schedule in-person and/or virtual tutoring
  • Unlimited 30-min sessions designed for maximum effectiveness
  • Click Learn More to check for availability in your area or see how you can help bring peer tutoring to your school
