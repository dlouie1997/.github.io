Tutoring Scheduler

Tutoring Scheduler

Forget about paper sign-in sheets and illegible feedbacks and notes from tutors. Need a tutor? Hop on to our online portal through your smartphone to request a tutor based on your needs and preferences for a 1-on-1, in-person tutoring session. During the booking process, students will be reminded to specify their questions and/or upload a picture of their homework. Tutors will be notified of a pending request via text and can check to see they are both available and actually know the content. Once they accept, both parties are all set and prepared for success!

Tutor Training

Tutor Training

While many high school students may be academically qualified, there’s much more to being a tutor. That’s why we prepare your tutors with the skills and tools to be patient and understanding and able to motivate students in addition to knowing how to ask the right questions to encourage inquiry-based learning. Through this opportunity, tutors gain a lot more than just service hours for their college apps!

Analytics & Dashboard

Analytics & Dashboard

From gathering tutoring insights to requesting additional sessions, our portal is an irreplaceable resource for all stakeholders.

  • Tutors can make notes for students to review at a later time
  • NHS sponsor can track earned service hours
  • Teachers/counselors can view student performance and engagement

Our online portal brings the school and community together through its well-designed dashboards.
