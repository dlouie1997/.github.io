PeerSquared Model

We are creating a sustainable and systematic peer tutoring model for schools, so one-on-one, in-person, peer tutoring can be provided by trained high school tutors as a free after school resource for all students. Together with technology, PeerSquared simplifies the experience for students while generating actionable insights for stakeholders.
