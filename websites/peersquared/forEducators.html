For Educators

You want to help student succeed, but have you unlocked the most potent resource in your classroom?

PeerSquared enables students to help each other through designing and building the most effective peer tutoring program for your school. With a PeerSquared Program, empower students to support one another and give more time to yourself to do what you do best.

Integrating Peer Tutoring into Your School

How can peer tutoring reach more students in your school? That is what we're here to help you answer. As educational consultants, we collaborate with teachers to find unique ways help them utilize peer tutoring for students. As recent students ourselves, we mobilize your student body to embrace peer tutoring. We will not stop until every at-risk student is reached!

Emphasizing the Social Quotient

Peer tutoring starts with caring and relatable peer tutors. PeerSquared's tutor training curriculum prepares tutors to leverage their passion to help others with their academic proficiency to effectively support their peers. Peer Tutors are friends, not teachers, and that's why students will feel comfortable going back whenever they need help.

Scheduling and Insights Made Easy

PeerSquared's custom Online Portal for peer tutoring allows students to easily schedule tutoring sessions and provide relevant information to tutors for the most effective session. After a session, tutors and students can leave feedback and notes for review at a later time, let teachers know how the session went, and offer insight to admins on how peer tutoring is helping their school.
